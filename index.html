Game Purpose

The goal is to beat the computer by selecting a move that wins according to the game's rules:

  • Rock beats Scissors (Rock crushes Scissors).
  • Scissors beats Paper (Scissors cut Paper).
  • Paper beats Rock (Paper covers Rock).
If one of the players reaches the chosen point count, they win!
If both players choose the same move, it's a tie! No one gets a point.

How to Play

  1. Press the Start button to begin the game.
  2. Choose the maximum points for the player and click Select.
  3. Choose your move:
    • Click on Rock, Paper, or Scissors to make your selection.
  4. The winner is determined based on the rules above.
